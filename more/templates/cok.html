Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites can use to make a user's experience more efficient. A cookie is stored on your device when you visit a website for the first time to allow the site to recognize you and remember your preferences on subsequent visits.

How do we use cookies?

  • Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of our website. They include, for example, cookies that allow you to log into secure areas of our website.
  • Analytical cookies: These cookies help us understand how you interact with our site, providing information about the pages you have visited and the time spent on the site. This helps us to improve our website and services.
  • Functional cookies: These cookies enable our website to remember your choices (such as your login details, language, or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

How can you control cookies?

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies, and to delete cookies already set. The following links provide information on how to adjust the cookie settings on some popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Apple Safari
